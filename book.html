The book

Prismatic Soul

The ancient technologies of sacred connection — an empirical guide for the modern seeker.

Written. With beta readers. Toward publication.

What it is

A structured, empirical guide to the practices that have been called spiritual, mystical, and esoteric across cultures — pulled out of dogma, hierarchy and story, and laid down again in language a modern nervous system can actually use.

The book builds a developmental map across seven Sections. It teaches the foundations — what self-awareness is, what nervous-system regulation actually does, what vibration and tone mean in practice — and then walks the same teachings through five tracks, so that the reader meets the work shaped to the way they are wired.

It is not a memoir. It is not a manifesto. It is a manual — written for the people whose minds and bodies have been quietly refusing the old packaging all along, and who want a real, body-first, sovereignty-first way in.

Who it's for

  • Nervous-system geeks who want the science and the practice.
  • Neurodiverse adults who have been failed by spiritual environments that rely on unspoken norms, performance of devotion, or vague language.
  • Parents raising ADHD or neurodiverse children, who want their own regulation practice while they support their kids'.
  • Anyone tired of dogma, hierarchy and bypass, and ready for an empirical guide to the functional content of the ancient technologies of sacred connection.

Your body takes a vividly imagined lemon as seriously as the real one. Which means everything else you imagine vividly enough, it will also take seriously. — Class 21 · Self-Talk and Visualization

The reaction is still there. You are simply no longer its employee. — Class 31 · Response Technologies

…the sudden conviction that you really ought to reorganise your sock drawer arriving just as you approach something important… — Class 44 · Asking for Help

When you watch someone else perform an action or experience an emotion, your brain fires the same neural patterns as if you were having the experience yourself. You are not merely observing. At the neurological level, you are participating. — Class 27 · Beyond the Skin

Bodies are not given to us for the journey; bodies are the journey. — Class 48 · Becoming

Most people notice their reaction, judge themselves for it, and then re-enter the same pattern with slightly more self-criticism attached. The loop tightens rather than opens. It is the spiritual equivalent of noticing you are stuck in traffic and responding by honking harder. — Class 29 · Validation, the Threshold and the Second Pivot

The Old Testament speaks of the consequences of the parents' actions visiting their children unto the third and fourth generation. The epigenetic research suggests this was not metaphor but lived observation. — Class 32 · Maintaining Causes: The Psychological Landscape

Read it before it's published

The manuscript is with readers right now, on the road to publication. There are two ways to get inside the book early.

Inner Circle — structured beta cohort

A small group (20–30 readers) who walk through the manuscript with me over a twelve-week window. You'll receive the full manuscript, take part in chapter-by-chapter check-ins, and answer specific questions tailored to each Part. Your feedback shapes the final book. You'll be named in the acknowledgements and receive a copy at publication.

Commitment — read the manuscript within 12 weeks, respond to check-ins as you go, attend one or two optional live conversations.
Spots — limited, by application.

You'll also be the first to hear about the launch, and invited (gently, never required) to leave a review when the book is released.

Inside the book

Seven Sections. A developmental arc.

  • Part One — Context and invitation. What this work is, what it isn't, and why now.
  • Part Two — The five tracks. Driver, Connector, Sustainer, Analyst, Integrator. The work, customised.
  • Parts Three through Seven — The core modules. Nervous-system regulation, focus and awareness, vibration and tone, response curation, clearing, ethics, intention. Each module met through every track, so the reader meets the work shaped to who they are.
