# Coaching

## A separate practice. A different door.

Alongside the work on this site, I run a separate coaching practice — focused on personal growth and living your values. It has its own home on the web, and its own rhythm.

---

### Mentoring or coaching?

The same hour can hold either mode. The two modalities are distinct.

**Mentoring** teaches tools, invites reflection, and tailors strategies. You leave with something to practice. *(That work lives [here →](mentoring.html).)*

**Coaching** listens deeply and reflects back where you are, and the strengths you already hold — so you can see more clearly how to move toward what matters.

If what you're looking for is the coaching side — particularly around personal growth, big moves, entrepreneurship, migration, or living your values — you can still contact me through this site, or visit the coaching site for my coaching reviews and resources.
