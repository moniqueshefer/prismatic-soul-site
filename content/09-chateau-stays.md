# Stays at the Chateau

## A quieter France, on slower time.

Through August the chateau opens its doors to summer guests — mostly family groups, often returning year after year. The grounds are the practice: the salt-water pool, the gardens and ponds, hammocks under the trees, the lake nearby, long evenings around the fire pit. Long meals, long light, the kind of silence that has crickets in it.

---

### When and for whom

- **August** — summer stays. Family groups and small private gatherings. **10-day minimum stay**, with a discount on stays of **two weeks or longer**.
- **May to late-July, and September to October** — reserved for our [week-long immersions →](workshops.html)

---

### Accommodation

Three buildings on the grounds:

- The chateau **gîte**
- Two neighbouring **farmhouses**

Capacity flexes with the group — couples, families, multi-generational gatherings, small private retreats. Quiet enough to actually rest. Big enough that everyone has somewhere to go.
