# Online Courses

## Demystifying Spirituality

### A foundation course in self-awareness and nervous-system regulation — taught in five tracks, so the work meets the way you're wired.

Coming soon — on Insight Timer and Udemy.

---

### What this course is

This is the foundation of everything. It takes the practices that have been called spiritual, mystical, and esoteric across cultures — and lays them out in a structure that makes empirical, contemplative, and practical sense.

The course teaches in two parts. The first is the foundation: what self-awareness actually is, what nervous-system regulation actually does, what vibration and tone mean in your own felt experience, and the developmental map the rest of the work hangs on. The second is the personalised work — five tracks, each one a different door into the same teachings, shaped to a different kind of nervous system and mind.

You will not be asked to believe anything. You will be asked to try things, notice what they do, and trust your own data.

---

### The structure

**Part One — Foundations.** Four classes:

1. Context and invitation — what this work is, what it isn't, and why now.
2. Who you are — the Track system.
3. A map you can actually use — the developmental framework.
4. What vibration actually is — subtle energy you can feel as tone, colour, or charge.

**Part Two — The Five Tracks.** Each track is a complete path through the core modules: nervous-system regulation, focus, awareness, vibration and tone, response curation, clearing, ethics, intention. You enter through the door that fits you. Eventually you'll be invited to walk through the others too.

---

### The five tracks

- **The Driver / Activator** — for the result-oriented, the high-output, the sceptical-until-it-works. Practice that starts with discharge and learns to receive.
- **The Connector / Feeler / Communicator** — for the empathic, the relationally attuned, the people who already feel a great deal. Practice that builds a stable interior and learns to choose tone rather than absorb it.
- **The Sustainer / Nurturer** — for the deeply present, the quietly reliable, the highly sensitive. Practice that activates as much as it settles, and learns to sustain the self as well as everyone else.
- **The Analyst / Observer** — for the systems thinkers, the precise, the cerebrally-dominant. Practice that earns the body through mechanism, and builds warmth as a deliberate skill.
- **The Integrator / Edge-Walker** — for the complex, the multiply neurodivergent, the don't-quite-fit-anywhere. Practice that holds all of you, and teaches that depth requires roots — even if yours look different.

You will recognise yourself in more than one. Most of us do.

### Who this course is for

- Nervous-system geeks who want the science *and* the practice.
- Neurodiverse adults whose previous attempts at meditation didn't quite work — and who suspect (correctly) that the problem wasn't them.
- Parents of ADHD or neurodiverse children who want their own foundation while they support their kids'.

You do not need a prior practice. You do not need to identify with a Track in advance. You do not need to know anything except that you are curious.
