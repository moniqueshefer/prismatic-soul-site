Privacy notice.

What we collect, why, and what we don't do with it.

Last updated: June 2026.

The short version

This site is run by Monique Shefer. We don't sell anything personal about you. We don't track you across other websites. We use a cookieless analytics tool to understand which pages people read. If you write to us or join the email list, we keep your name and email so we can reply or send you what you asked for. You can ask us to delete that at any time.

What we collect

  • Information you give us — your name and email address if you write through the contact form or sign up to the email list, plus whatever you choose to share in your message.
  • Information collected automatically — anonymous, aggregate page-view data via Cloudflare Web Analytics. No cookies. No personal identifiers stored.

How we use it

  • To reply to your messages.
  • To deliver the lead-magnet you asked for (e.g. the Survey A result + welcome series).
  • To send the email newsletter (only if you opted in).
  • To understand which pages are read and improve the site.

Who we share it with

The minimum required to operate. Specifically:

  • MailerLite — to deliver email if you join the list.
  • Formspree — to relay contact-form messages to my inbox.
  • Cloudflare — for site hosting and analytics.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data with anyone.

Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, at any time. Write to monique@prismatic-soul.com with the request.

Cookies

This site doesn't use tracking cookies. The only thing stored in your browser is a single preference (whether you've accepted the cookie banner, so we don't show it to you twice).

Questions

Write to monique@prismatic-soul.com.
